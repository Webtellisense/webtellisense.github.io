10 Essential Tips for Small Business Online Success in Eket

  • Webtellisense Team
  • May 15, 2023

As an Eket business owner, establishing a strong online presence is no longer optional - it's essential for survival. At Webtellisens, we've helped dozens of local businesses thrive online. Here are our top 10 actionable tips:

1. Claim Your Google Business Profile

Over 80% of local searches start on Google. A complete, optimized profile makes you visible when customers search for services in Eket. Include photos, accurate hours, and respond to reviews.

2. Build a Mobile-Friendly Website

With most Nigerian internet users on mobile, your site must work perfectly on phones. Ensure fast loading speeds and easy navigation - we've seen businesses increase leads by 150% after mobile optimization.

"After Webtellisens redesigned our site, our bakery saw online orders triple within 2 months." - Mary K., Eket Delights

3. Leverage Local Social Media Groups

Active in communities like "Eket Business Network" on Facebook? Share valuable content (not just ads) to build trust. A local mechanic gained 30 new customers monthly this way.

4. Collect Customer Reviews

Nigerian consumers trust online reviews as much as personal recommendations. Politely ask satisfied customers to leave feedback on Google or Facebook.
