our working process

How we work

After a contract is signed we send a small questionnaire about your business and what you offer, then we expand on that content and write the entire site’s content for you, create the design, get approval, then we build. Generally takes 2-3 weeks from start to finish.

step 1

fill questionnaire

We’ll email you a short questionnaire (less than 10 questions) about your business, services, and what makes you unique. Once you complete it, leave the rest to us—we’ll handle everything from there!

step 2

design approval

Next, we’ll present the first draft of your website via a video screen share. You’ll have the opportunity to review and suggest any changes to ensure the design reflects your vision perfectly.

step 3

development

After you approve the design, we’ll bring it to life. Our team will code the website and set everything up. Before launching, we’ll share a demo link so you can review and provide feedback, ensuring everything is ready to go live.
