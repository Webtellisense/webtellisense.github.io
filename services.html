Common questions

Things business owners usually ask before getting started.

Which service should I start with?
Start where your biggest pain is right now. No website yet? Frontline. Your brand looks unprofessional? Swagger. You need customers quickly and have a budget for ads? Fireshot. You are not sure? Book a free consultation and we will tell you honestly which service your business needs first — and which ones can wait.
Do you work with businesses outside Eket?
Yes. While Webtellisense is based in Eket, Akwa Ibom, we work with businesses across Nigeria. Digital marketing is location-independent on our end — what matters is that your customers can find you online wherever they are.
How is Webtellisense different from hiring a freelancer?
A freelancer delivers a file. Webtellisense builds a system. Your website, SEO, social media, ads, and conversion funnel are all connected — and they are all being managed by one team that knows the full picture. Freelancers solve individual problems. We solve for growth.
How long does it take to see results from SEO?
For local SEO in Eket and Akwa Ibom, you will typically start seeing movement in rankings within 60 to 90 days. This is why Deeproots is a retainer — SEO compounds over time. The businesses that start now and stay consistent are the ones ranking at the top a year from now.
Can I upgrade from one service to Heroes later?
Yes — that is exactly how the architecture is designed. Every service you start with is a step toward Heroes. When you are ready to stop juggling separate vendors and hand everything to one team, the transition is clean because we already know your business.
What makes Webtellisense right for Nigerian SMEs specifically?
We are built for the way Nigerian customers discover and buy. WhatsApp funnels, Google Business Profiles, local SEO, social media in Nigerian business context — none of this is retrofitted from a foreign playbook. Our digital marketing services are designed for the Nigerian SME reality from the ground up.
