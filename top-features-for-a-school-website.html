7 Must-Have Features for School Websites in Akwa Ibom

  • Webtellisense Team
  • March 19, 2024

Your school's website is often the first impression for prospective parents. After designing websites for schools across Eket and Uyo, we've identified these essential features:

1. Clear Admission Information

Include downloadable forms, requirements, and deadlines. St. Mary's Primary saw 40% more inquiries after simplifying their admissions section.

2. Mobile-Friendly Calendar

Parents should easily access term dates, exams, and events. Sync with Google Calendar for automatic updates.

3. Faculty Directory

Showcase your qualified staff with bios and photos to build trust. This helped Heritage Academy attract better teaching candidates.

"Our new website reduced front office queries by 60%, giving staff more time for students." - Principal Udoh, Excel Nursery School
